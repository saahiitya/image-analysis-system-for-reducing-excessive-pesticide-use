## 📊 Comprehensive Disease Database

### Tomato Diseases
1. **Early Blight**
   - Pesticides: Mancozeb, Copper Oxychloride, Propiconazole, Azoxystrobin
   - Price Range: ₹120-1200 per unit
   
2. **Late Blight**
   - Pesticides: Metalaxyl+Mancozeb, Cymoxanil+Mancozeb, Dimethomorph+Mancozeb
   - Price Range: ₹280-450 per kg
   
3. **Bacterial Wilt**
   - Pesticides: Streptocycline+Tetracycline, Copper Sulphate, Kasugamycin
   - Price Range: ₹150-800 per unit

### Brinjal (Eggplant) Diseases
1. **Fruit and Shoot Borer**
   - Pesticides: Bt (Bacillus thuringiensis), Emamectin Benzoate, Chlorantraniliprole
   - Price Range: ₹400-3200 per unit
   
2. **Little Leaf Disease**
   - Pesticides: Imidacloprid, Thiamethoxam, Acetamiprid
   - Price Range: ₹850-1800 per unit
   
3. **Damping Off**
   - Pesticides: Captan+Hexaconazole, Carbendazim+Mancozeb, Fosetyl Aluminium
   - Price Range: ₹220-750 per kg

### Capsicum (Bell Pepper) Diseases
1. **Anthracnose**
   - Pesticides: Mancozeb, Carbendazim, Azoxystrobin
   - Price Range: ₹180-1200 per unit
   
2. **Powdery Mildew**
   - Pesticides: Wettable Sulphur, Triadimefon, Hexaconazole
   - Price Range: ₹120-680 per unit
   
3. **Thrips**
   - Pesticides: Fipronil, Imidacloprid, Spinosad
   - Price Range: ₹850-2800 per unit
